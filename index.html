About Us

μDIE is the name of microelectronics and IoT workgroup in the Electric Engineering Department, in the Universidad Católica del Uruguay.

Our main areas of research are:

  • Analog and mixed-signal integrated circuit design for low-power applications
  • Application Specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs) for implantable medical divices
  • MOS transistor noise modeling and low noise designs
  • IoT applications for the agro-industry
  • Integrated optoelectronic devices design and characterization
  • Photodetector Application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) for pattern correlation and alternative imaging

μDIE first started in 2005 and has the experience and a basic tool set for the design and characterization of low power and low frequency ASICs, In the last 20 years, its members have fabricated >20 ASICs, published 25 journal and 100 conference papers, while participating >15 funded research projects.



μDIE has organized and co-organized several international conferences:

    3rd IEEE Conference in AgriFood Electronics (CAFE). October 8 - 10, 2025, Montevideo, Uruguay.

  • LASCAS 2024

    15th IEEE Latin American Symposium on Circuits and Systems (LASCAS) and the XXX Iberchip Workshop. February 27 - March 01, 2024 in Punta del Este, Uruguay.

  • LASCAS 2015

    6th IEEE Latin American Symposium on Circuits and Systems (LASCAS) and the XXI Iberchip Workshop. February 24 - 27, 2015 in Montevideo, Uruguay.

  • EAMTA 2010

    5th Argentine School of Micro-Nanoelectronics, Technology and Applications (EAMTA 2010) and its associated Conference (CAMTA-CUMTA 2010). October 1 - 9, 2010 in Montevideo, Uruguay.
