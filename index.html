About Us

μDIE is the Microelectronics and IoT research group within the Department of Engineering at the Universidad Católica del Uruguay.

Main research areas

  • Analog and mixed-signal IC design for low-power applications
  • ASICs for implantable medical devices
  • MOS transistor noise modeling and low-noise design
  • IoT solutions for agro-industry
  • Integrated optoelectronic devices
  • Photodetector ASICs for imaging

  • EAMTA 2010

    Argentine School of Micro-Nanoelectronics. Montevideo, Uruguay.

Founded in 2005, μDIE has developed strong expertise in low-power ASIC design. The group has fabricated 20+ ASICs, published 25 journal papers and 100 conference papers, and participated in 15+ funded research projects.
